## RF fundamentals
* **Radio frequency (RF):** is any of the electromagnetic wave frequencies that lie in the range starting from around 3 kHz to 300 GHz
* **Purpose of RF signal:** We use these signals or waves for wireless broadcasting or communication using antennas, trasmitters and receivers
* Many types of wireless devices make use of RF fields. Cellphones, radio and television broadcast stations, Wi-Fi and Bluetooh, satellite communications systems, and two-way radios all operate in the RF spectrum
