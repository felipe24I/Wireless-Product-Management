* **isotropic:** means having identical physical properties in all directions.
*  **isotropic antenna:** is a theoretical, ideal antenna that radiates or receives radio wave energy equally in a perfect sphere (all directions).
*  **Isotropic Radiation Intensity:** The power radiated per unit solid angle by an isotropic antenna.

* **dBi (decibels relative to isotropic)** is a relative unit that measures the forward gain of an antenna compared to a theoretical, ideal isotropic antenna. It quantifies how effectively an antenna focuses radio energy in a single direction rather than scattering it in a perfect sphere
