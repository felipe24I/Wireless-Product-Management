## Different types of Noise
* **Thermal noise:** thermal agitation of charge carriers -  typically electrons - in a conductor

<img width="283" height="171" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c58fe2e6-edbb-4fe6-8af5-b96d599429f7" />

* **Shot noise:** This form of noise that arises from the time-dependent fluctuations in electrical current (Originates from the discrete nature of electric charge)
* **Phase noise:** phase noise is phase variation (phase noise is a form of RF noise that is visible on radio frequency, and other signals. It appears in the form of phase jitter or perturbations on the signal)

**Note:** Phase jitter -> the pulses of an electronic or optical signal arrive slightly too early or too late
